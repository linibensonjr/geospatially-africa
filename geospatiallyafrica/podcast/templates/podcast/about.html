About Us

Geospatial Africa podcast started in April 2021 and was originally called Geospatially Podcast, we decided to change the name to Geospatially Africa podcast to make it more African centric in the sense that it is a podcast that holds interesting conversation about the industry in Africa and around the world. Currently one of the very few media outlets showcasing Afro-centric geospatial talents, expertise and innovations globally, we strive to drive the narrative of the immense potential within the African geospatial industry, while exploring how it is contributing to Africa's individual and collective development, across all indicators
